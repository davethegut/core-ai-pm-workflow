**Q1: How does this align with our strategy?**
[Explain how this initiative supports the team/company's strategic priorities and long-term vision.]

**Q3: What are the key success metrics (KPIs)?**
[List 3-5 measurable outcomes. Include targets and timeframes. Example: "10% adoption within 90 days of GA."]

**Q4: What are the biggest risks?**
[Identify 2-4 risks — technical, market, adoption, regulatory. For each, describe the mitigation plan.]

[Describe phases — private beta, public beta, GA. Include timelines and gating criteria for each phase.]

**Q6: What are the dependencies?**
[List cross-team, infrastructure, or third-party dependencies. Note which are on the critical path.]

**Q7: What is the estimated cost?**
[Engineering effort (person-months), infrastructure costs, ongoing operational costs. Separate build vs. run costs.]

**Q8: What is the timeline?**
[Key milestones with dates. Include design complete, dev complete, beta, GA. Note what's committed vs. aspirational.]

**Q9: What are we explicitly NOT doing?**
[List scope boundaries and deferred items. Explain why they were cut and when they might be revisited.]

**Q10: How will we measure whether to continue, pivot, or kill this?**
