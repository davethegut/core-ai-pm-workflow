Doc Reviewer

Get CTO or GM-level feedback on your documents before the meeting. Format-aware, persona-aware review based on Kent Beck's 3X framework.

Saves 30–60 min

What It Does

This is not generic “make it clearer” AI feedback. The skill detects your document format—one-pager, PRFAQ, PRD, decision doc—and applies format-specific standards so the critique matches what reviewers actually look for. On top of that, it layers persona-specific critique: a CTO lens for engineering simplicity and delivery risk, and a GM lens for product–market fit, phase clarity, and decision quality.

You get feedback you can act on before the meeting, instead of discovering gaps when an exec asks the one question you didn’t anticipate.

Review Personas

CTO Review

Focuses on feature fit versus stack complexity, the green-field fallacy (“we’ll just rebuild it”), whether the approach is as simple as it can be, and phasing risk—can you ship incrementally or are you betting the quarter on a big bang?

GM Review — Concept

Grounds the idea in Kent Beck’s 3X framework: clarity of phase (Explore / Expand / Extract), whether the problem has real gravity for customers, and concrete adoption mechanics—not just “users will love it.”

GM Review — Decision

Stress-tests decision clarity (what exactly are we choosing?), ownership and accountability, reversibility if we’re wrong, and opportunity cost—what we’re not doing because we chose this path.

Example Prompts

Review this one-pager
Give me CTO feedback on this doc

How It Works

Get document content

Accepts a Google Docs link (via integration where configured) or text you paste directly, so the review runs on the real artifact—not a summary you typed from memory.

Detect format

Classifies the doc as Concept, Problem/Solution, Decision, PRFAQ, PRD, or general—then applies the right rubric for structure, depth, and missing sections.

Detect persona

Infers or uses your stated lens: CTO (engineering and delivery), GM (business and strategy), or a balanced general review when you don’t specify.

Deliver layered feedback

Outputs format-aware notes (what’s wrong with the doc as that type of doc) plus persona-aware critique (what that leader would push on), prioritized so you can revise quickly.
